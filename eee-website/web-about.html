Our Vision

To produce globally competent electrical and electronics engineers for addressing the needs of humanity with ethical values.

Our Mission
  • To empower the students with knowledge in recent trends in Electrical and Electronics Engineering.
  • To impart technical skills to resolve industrial problems through innovative teaching learning practices and research.
