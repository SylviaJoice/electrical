Division of Electrical and Electronics Engineering

INTRODUCTION

The Division of Electrical and Electronics Engineering was established in 1994. It is well equipped with highly commendable facilities and is effectively guided by a set of devoted and diligent staff members. The Division has also signed MoA with industries like SIEMENS Centre of Excellence for "Automation and Drives" – Siemens Authorized Training Centre for the entire Southern India, supported by Siemens (German multinational conglomerate company headquartered in Munich and the largest industrial manufacturing company in Europe), SALZER Electronics Limited (which is one of the leading industries in Electrical Protection Device and Modern Sensor Manufacturing in India), SELENS Elevators Pvt. Ltd to provide Industrial Certification Training. Students are offered with innovative projects, in-plant trainings and internships through this collaboration.

Graduates of the Program

  • ✔ PEO I: Pursue a successful career by demonstrating the knowledge acquired in Electrical Engineering.
  • ✔ PEO II: Exhibit technical skills in solving real-world problems.
  • ✔ PEO III: Engage in professional practice with ethical values and lifelong learning.
  • ✔ Engineering knowledge: Apply math, science, and engineering fundamentals.
  • ✔ Problem analysis: Identify and analyze engineering problems.
  • ✔ PSO 1: Solid foundation in math and electrical engineering.
  • ✔ PSO 2: Design and analyze electrical systems.
  • ✔ PSO 3: Simulate machines and automation systems.
  • ✔ PSO 4: Implement and test analog/digital systems.
  • ✔ Attainment measured using assessments and project outcomes.
  • ✔ Reviewed annually for improvement.
