Job Opportunities in Electrical and Electronics Engineering

B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering opens surplus of opportunities in both government and private organizations. The UPSC Board for recruitment conducts the Indian Engineering Service (IES) examination for recruiting electrical engineers into various government departments. Research organizations like the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO), Defense Research and Development Organization(DRDO) and Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre hire electrical and electronics engineering graduates for various job roles. National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC), Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL), Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL), Coal India Limited (CIL), etc are some of the PSU where undergraduate and master degree holders in electrical engineering can try for lucrative job. Coming to jobs in private organizations, EEE students have plenty of opportunities in industries like electrical, electronics, communications, telecommunications, information technology and instrumentation. Opportunities are available in IT firms like Infosys, Wipro, and Accenture. Engineering graduates in Electrical and Electronics Engineering have adequate higher study options, both in India as well as abroad. M.E/M.Tech courses (in India) are offered in subjects of electrical engineering, power electronics, power systems, electronic design and technology, and electrical energy systems. International Universities offer courses such as M.S in Electrical Power Systems, Electrical Energy Conversion Systems, Master of Research in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Master of Engineering Science (Electrical Engineering).
